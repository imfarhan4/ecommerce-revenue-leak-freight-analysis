# E-Commerce Revenue Leak & Freight Cost Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce business performance using SQL, Python, and Power BI to uncover insights into revenue trends, revenue leakage, and freight cost patterns. The analysis was conducted on **115K+ orders**, generating insights from **19.93M in revenue**, **146.7K in revenue lost due to cancellations**, and **2.32M in freight costs**.

## Project Workflow

### SQL

* Created a consolidated analytical view from multiple e-commerce tables
* Calculated total revenue
* Identified the highest revenue-generating product category
* Identified the top revenue-generating state
* Analyzed monthly revenue trends

### Python

* Performed data cleaning and preprocessing
* Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA)
* Analyzed revenue trends, cancellations, customer reviews, and freight costs
* Generated business insights and visualizations

### Power BI

Built an interactive dashboard consisting of:

#### Revenue Overview

Analyzes revenue trends, order performance, customer ratings, product categories, and regional sales.

#### Revenue Leak Analysis

Identifies revenue losses caused by order cancellations, delivery delays, and customer dissatisfaction.

#### Freight Analysis

Evaluates shipping costs across products and regions to understand freight cost patterns and operational efficiency.

## Key Insights

* Total Revenue: 19.93M
* Orders Analyzed: 115K+
* Revenue Lost Due to Cancellations: 146.7K
* Total Freight Cost: 2.32M
* Freight Cost Share: 11.6% of Revenue
* Average Customer Rating: 4.03/5
